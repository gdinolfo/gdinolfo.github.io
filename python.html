Python Coding Data Analysis



Project Overview:  In this project, I was are tasked with creating a Python script for analyzing the financial records of a fictitious company. The information was given in a CSV set of financial data. The dataset is composed of two columns: `Date` and `Profit/Losses`.

Approach:  I created a Python script that analyzes the records to calculate each of the following:

    • The total number of months included in the dataset
    • The total net amount of "Profit/Losses" over the entire period
    • The average change in "Profit/Losses" between months over the entire period
    • The greatest increase in profits (date and amount) over the entire period
    • The greatest decrease in losses (date and amount) over the entire period

Conclusions:  My analysis was summarized into the total months, dollar amount and changes in profits for the file provided.


Python with Pandas Coding Data Analysis



Project Overview:  In this project, I was are tasked with of analyzing the data for a fictitious fantasy game Heroes of Pymoli. The information was given in a CSV set for various purchase information and demographics.

Approach:  I created Jupyter Notebook with Python and Pandas to analyzes the records to calculate each of the following:

    • Total Number of Players
    • Number of Unique Items
    • Average Purchase Price
    • Total Number of Purchases
    • Total Revenue
    • Gender and Age Demographics
    • Top Spenders and Most Popular Items
    • Most Profitable Items

Conclusions: 

    • The large majority of games are purchased by males at 84.03% vs female at only 14.06% but males spend less on an average purchase price.
    • The largest age range to purchase games are 20-24 year olds but 35-39 year olds spend more per purchase.
    • The top 3 most popular games were also the most profitable.
